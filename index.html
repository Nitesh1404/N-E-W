Big Kharkana.
Bigger Commitment.

We are a precision machining kharkana based in Mumbai, Byculla, Mazagaon, founded in 2003. Our workshop is powered by skilled workers who work together like a family. Since 2003, we have been delivering precision lathe work, drilling, CNC and VMC machining to clients across industries.

What makes us different is not just our machines — it's our people. Our workers are experienced, dedicated, and treat every job with the same care they would give to their own work. We've built a culture where quality isn't checked at the end — it's built in at every step.

Our big kharkana (workshop) is equipped with multiple lathe machines, drilling machines, and multiple CNC machines to handle both bulk and urgent jobs efficiently.

Whether you need a one-off custom component or a recurring production batch, we deliver on time, every time — that is our promise and our track record.
